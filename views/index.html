Instructions

With EventBored you can post events that are going on around the Bay Area to spread the word and get more people involved. All you have to do is:

  • click on our EventBored Board
  • write your name and contact info
  • write your event details such as time, place, description, and purpose

People who see your event and want to participate can then write their names and contact info in the comments section so that you can then get in
touch and gather people for your event. Now get on out there and have some fun!
