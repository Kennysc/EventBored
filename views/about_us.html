About Us

Mission Statement

We are a group of young adults who wanted to give the residents of Richmond a place where they can connect with others and help their community. Our goal for our users is to create a collaborative community of citizens who help promote, organize and include other like-minded people to come together and make our city a safe and open environment that fosters creativity and ingenuity.
